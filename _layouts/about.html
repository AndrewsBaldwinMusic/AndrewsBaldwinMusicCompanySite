About Andrews-Baldwin Music Company

Andrews-Baldwin Music Company officially opened in 2012, but founder, Lequita Andrews-Baldwin, has been sharing her love of music long before that. The Music Company provides multiple lessons for students of all ages. The focus is to help each musician develop their gifts. Multiple instructors help teach ABMC students and provide opportinities to showcase their talents.
