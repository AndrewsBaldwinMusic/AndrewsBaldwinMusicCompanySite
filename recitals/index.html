Music Recital

The Music Recital is a performace held each year for students to showcase their talent. All students who have taken lessons in the Winter/Spring session are asked to participate. Parents, family members, and friends are encouraged to come. Afterwards students are presented with awards from the instructors and all are invited to enjoy refreshments. Make sure to bring your cameras!

Date of 2018 Recital: April 27th

Location: St. Anthony Cathedral Basilica School Cafeteria, Beaumont, Texas

Time: 7pm

Dress Code: Sunday Best
