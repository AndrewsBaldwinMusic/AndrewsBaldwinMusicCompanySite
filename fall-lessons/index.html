Fall Lessons

Fall Lessons span the months of September, October, and November. Lessons are held virtually on Google Classrooms or a privately linked Youtube video. Live Private Lessons take place exclusively on Friday by schedule availability. Each weekly instruction will run approximately 30 minutes. The registration fee is $30 and $50 for late registration. Current Lessons offered are Guitar, Drums, Keyboard/Piano, Bass, Vocals, Mandolin, and Ukulele. Students of private lessons will be contacted by email or by phone number provided to confirm lesson dates. The monthly fee is $100.
