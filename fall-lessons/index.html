Fall Lessons

Fall Lessons span the months of September, October, and November. Lessons are held on Fridays exclusively, with make-up lessons held on the last Friday of the month. Private Lessons take place at the business house, 2107 Avenue A in Beaumont, Texas. The registration fee is $30 and $50 for late registration. Individual lessons are held on Friday by schedule availability. Current Lessons offered are Guitar, Drums, Keyboard, Banjo, Bass, Vocals, Mandolin, Cello, and Ukelele. Students of private lessons will be contacted by the number given on the registration form to confirm lesson dates. The monthly fee is $100.
