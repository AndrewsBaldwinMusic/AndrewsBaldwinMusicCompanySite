Winter/Spring Lessons

Winter/Spring Lessons span the months of January, Febuary, and March. At the end of this course, students are able to participate at the Music Recital in April. Group lessons are held on Fridays exclusively. Lessons take place at the business house, 2107 Avenue A in Beaumont. The registration fee is $30 and $50 for late registration. Currently, the group lessons offered are for Guitar, Ukulele Keyboard/ Piano, Vocals, and Drums. The monthly fee is $60. Individual lessons are held on Friday as well by schedule availability. Current Lessons offered are Guitar, Keyboard Drums, Banjo, Bass, Vocals, Mandolin and Ukulele. Students of private lessons will be contacted by the number given on the registration form to confirm lesson dates. The monthly fee is $100.
