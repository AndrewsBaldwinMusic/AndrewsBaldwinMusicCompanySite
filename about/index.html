Mrs. Lequita Andrews-Baldwin

Mrs. Lequita Andrews-Baldwin is the owner of Andrews-Baldwin Music Company. Mrs. Baldwin has been teaching music to students since the late 70’s. She teaches Guitar, Bass, Drums, Keyboard and Songwriting. She is also a singer/songwriter who has produced 3 CD’s of her original Christian music with the group TRUST. Currently, Lequita Baldwin’s music videos can be enjoyed on her YouTube channel. Lequita is excited to resume in-person lessons in Beaumont and is adding classes in Clear Lake. Online classes are still available upon request.

Mr. Charley Baldwin

Mr. Charley Baldwin was born in Houston in 1961. He began playing guitar at age 10. He, for the most part, has been self taught. He recorded in Houston on both secular and religious albums. At age 11, he began playing for St. Francis of Assisi Church in Houston, and was responsible for the music at the 5pm Mass, from the time he was 15 until 18. Later he joined the Instrument of Peace Gospel Choir and performed along with other Houston choirs in concert with gospel artist Edwin Hawkins. Chuck, as he is known, recorded with a Seminary group from Corpus Christi, and played in St. Mary Seminary’s jazz band. He went on to provide music for the Josephite house of studies in New Orleans. While teaching in New Orleans, Chuck met the love of his life and began playing lead guitar for the Andrews sisters in 1990. He married Lequita Andrews in 1991 and has played for her in the band Trust. Over the last 29 years, he has taught guitar, and performed at various Churches. His work can be heard on the YouTube channel “Lequita Baldwin” Come learn to play guitar from someone who really loves music.

Mrs. Sylvia Watkins-Richard

Sylvia Watkins-Richard is a native of Lafayette, Louisiana. She is a graduate of Northside High School and earned her BME (Bachelor of Music Education) degree from the University of Louisiana-Lafayette in 1980. She taught in the Lafayette Parish School System for 33 years as a Classroom Music Teacher and Choral Instructor. She has worked in the music ministry beginning at the age of 12 years old as pianist for her local church (Gethsemane Church of God in Christ) youth choir. Over the years she served as music minister at her father’s church (Rev. Charles C. Watkins-Triune Church of God in Christ – Franklin, LA), under Rev. Frank Ford at Community Chapel Church of God in Christ in Opelousas, LA; and presently at Acts of Love Church of God in Christ in Scott, LA where Rev. Abram Freeman serves as pastor. She also serves as pianist for the Acadiana Ecumenical Choir and Tri-Parish Chapter of the Gospel Music Workshop of America. She has taught private piano since 1976 – Grace Hamilton Academy of the Arts, Joseph Breaux Music Studio, Bottoms Up Art Academy. She brings to her instruction a love for music and sharing it with her students.
