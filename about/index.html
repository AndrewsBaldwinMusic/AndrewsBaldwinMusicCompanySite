Ms. Lequita Andrews-Baldwin

Lequita Andrews-Baldwin is the owner of Andrews-Baldwin Music Company. Nrs. Baldwin has been teaching music to students since the late 70's. She teaches Guitar, Bass, Drums, and Keyboard. She is also a singer/songwriter and has produced 3 CD's of her original music with the Christian group, TRUST. Currently, Mrs. Baldwin organizes the choior music and plays for Saturday and Sunday Mass at Our Lady of Victory in Sour Lake.

Mrs. Felicia Andrews-Runnels

Felicia Andrews-Runnels teaches Vocal lessons and Performance/Recording lessons. She has been teaching Vocals with ABMC since its creation. Mrs. Runnels was raised in a musical family and has been singing her whole life. She loves sharing her gift of song and teaching others to use their gift to make a joyful noise to God.

Mr. Charley Baldwin

Mr. Baldwin began playing guitar at thirteen. He has played on four CD's. He's worked with the Hard Road Band, and he's been with TRUST since 1991. He is thrilled to teach guitar.

Ms. Philomena Baldwin

A former student of the music company now turned teacher, Ms. Baldwin has been teaching music lessons on and off for about 7 years. She recently graduated with a degree in Fine Arts. Ms. Baldwin teaches Drum lessons and 2D Animation lessons.

Mrs. Renee Tolin

Mrs. Tolin teaches Piano/Keyboard lessons and Flute lessons. She currently teaches at St. Anthony Cathedral Basilica School. She has been teaching Keyboard lessons with ABMC since its creation.
