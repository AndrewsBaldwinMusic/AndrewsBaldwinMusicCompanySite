Mrs. Lequita Andrews-Baldwin

Lequita Andrews-Baldwin is the owner of Andrews-Baldwin Music Company. Mrs. Baldwin has been teaching music to students since the late 70's. She teaches Guitar, Bass, Drums, and Keyboard. She is also a singer/songwriter and has produced 3 CD's of her original music with the Christian group, TRUST. Currently, Mrs. Baldwin organizes choir music and plays for Saturday and Sunday Mass at Our Lady of Victory in Sour Lake.

Mrs. Felicia Andrews-Runnels

Felicia Andrews-Runnels teaches Vocal lessons. She has been teaching Vocals with ABMC since its creation. Mrs. Runnels was raised in a musical family and has been singing her whole life. She loves sharing her gift of song and teaching others to use their gift to make a joyful noise to God.

Mr. Charley Baldwin

Mr. Baldwin began playing guitar at thirteen. He has played on four CD's. He's worked with the Hard Road Band, and he's been with TRUST since 1991 and ABMC since it's opening. He is thrilled to teach guitar.

Ms. Philomena Baldwin

A former student of the music company now turned teacher, Ms. Baldwin has been teaching music lessons on and off for about 8 years. She graduated with a degree in Fine Arts and works as a freelance artist. Ms. Baldwin teaches Drum lessons.

Mrs. Tresa Tolin

Mrs. Tolin originally took up guitar lessons with Mrs. Baldwin to stay in tune with her grandchildren. She discovered a joy and love for music that she likes to share with others. She's passionate about learning all kinds of instruments such as banjo, mandolin, ukulele, and piano.

Mrs. April Knod

Mrs. Knod played trumpet for 7 years. 3 of those years she was in the marching band and concert band. She will be teaching drumline this summer.

Mr. Lance Knod

Mr. Knod played baritone sax for 6 years and alto sax for 1 year. He played bass drum on occasion in the Nederland heritage festival. He also played snare drum in the Snare and Bugle Corp in the Navy. He will be teaching drumline this summer.
